Frequently Asked Questions

Life happens! For the Endowment plan, you can take a contribution break (re-date once), reduce premiums to KES 2,000, or ICEA LION can take an Automatic Premium Loan on your behalf to keep the policy active. For other plans, a grace period applies — contact us and we'll help you navigate the options.

If you're employed, inform your HR department about your policy and they'll apply a 15% monthly tax relief on your premiums. If self-employed, claim it directly from KRA. The maximum relief is KES 60,000 per year — real money back in your pocket.

Your life cover is active immediately after the contractual agreements are finalized and your first premium is paid. For Endowment and Whole of Life plans, the full Sum Assured is payable from day one.

Yes! With the Endowment With Profits plan, you can take a policy loan from year 3 (after 36 months of contributions). The loan amount depends on the policy's current value, not the Sum Assured. This is a great way to access funds without surrendering your policy.

This is one of the most powerful features of Usomi Bora. If you (the parent/guardian) pass away during the policy term, ICEA LION will continue paying your premiums on your behalf — and your child will still receive the full benefits at maturity. Their education is protected no matter what.

Endowment With Profits is a long-term plan (10–20 years) focused on wealth building with bonuses from investments, and includes strong life insurance. InvestSure is shorter-term, market-linked with declared returns (4–12%), and offers a capital guarantee plus a free KES 150K life cover. InvestSure is ideal for specific financial goals; Endowment is better for long-term wealth creation.
